Ingredients:
 - 1 cup quinoa
 - rape oil
 - 2 green onions
 - 1/2 onion
 - 3 tablespoons sweet corn
 - 3 tablespoons peas
 - 1 red pepper
 - 3 tablespoons tomato puree
 - 2 teaspoons vegetable stock
 - salt
 - pepper
 - 100 g feta cheese

Directions:
 1. Roast the vegetables in rape oil
 2. Add the quinoa and the tomato puree
 3. Add the vegetable stock and 2 cups of water
 4. Cook until the water has disappeared
 5. Add salt, pepper and feta
